Video Viewer

So, you are the visual learner type, huh? Here you can watch all the videos that I've recorded for my friends to help them study for exams. These videos are first recorded during the midst of the COVID-19 pandemic, and you can see the quality of the videos keep improving as the semester goes on. I hope you enjoy watching these videos as much as I enjoyed making them.

It is important to note that these videos are not meant to be used as a replacement for attending lectures. Some contents in these videos may be outdated at the time of your viewing. Please check with your lecture notes to make sure that the content is still relevant. Also, you can use pdf files that I've provided to follow along with the videos. Some videos may contain errors and typos, so take a look at video descriptions to see if there are any corrections first before using them.

Remark: ANY USE OF THESE FILES FOR ANY PURPOSE OTHER THAN PERSONAL USE IS STRICTLY PROHIBITED IN EVERY CIRCUMSTANCES. These videos are provided for free, no money is being made. The only reason I'm providing these files is to help my fellow students. If you are caught using any of these files for any other purpose, I will not hesitate to take legal action against you.
